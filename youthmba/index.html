YouthMBA - Projects Shopping Platform

YouthMBA helps you start an excellent and wonderful life.

YouthMBA is an education council. What makes it different from other education and training council is that it only services for children of those elite people in commercial circle, provide the children with professional / international bussiness / economy knowledge and all kinds of international projects to expand children's horizon. So this site is the shopping platform for users of YouthMBA. We provide all features and interaction which an e-commerce system should have. People can conveniently view and buy any commercial and international project of YouthMBA there.

My role

I worked with the my interest group partner and learned my client's requirments, to design the website from the ground up. I designed the interactive proccess all through the site and the UI.
