About the Project

TangSec is a information security company which is originated from WooYun Commuity and provides security products and services to various clients. There are three main products aiming to help clients to solve their security issues in a fast and effective way - Tangscan / Ce / Burp. And we need to convey our values of company and products to other potential clients and security lovers.

My Role

I was the designer in this project. I got the main values and expectation from CEO and looked for the best way to show them and communicate those values and goals, collaborated and talked with colleagues from marketing apartment and tried to understand our products and values more deeply. Combed the Information Structure and wrote copywriting of all features and products which would be presented in official site. What's more, I was responsible for the logo design, interaction and visual design of this website. And drew icon set and illustrations for TangSec as well.
