Zhihu for Android

2013.7 – 2014.2 | Product Design | UX Design | Android UI Design | Live Version

I was the Product designer, also worked as an interaction designer and visual designer on this project, working with the mobile team of Zhihu, a bunch of incredibly qualified and jolly people, on a tight deadline.

Project Description

Zhihu is the Q&A Social platform, and Zhihu for Android is the mobile app of Zhihu.com on Android platform. People could talk about any professional, academic or interesting topics there. With over 2 million registered users and 200 thousand active daily users, it is one of the most potencial and professional Q&A social platform in China. We created a central place giving users an oppotunity to speak out their own views or ideas and communicate with other people with a serious and professional attitude.

Real Android Design

All pages followed the Guide of Android Design and our heart.

In this Project, we wanted to update some parts of Zhihu App, including the information structure, compose type, icons and buttons etc, in order to improve users' especially the new users' experience.

In the beginning, I analyzed new users' behaviors and habits via Email, Zhihu.com and People in my daily life. I chatted with users or potential users online & offline, collecting their feedbacks. After that I went to CBD and interviewed several potential users. When they used the old version App, I observed the interaction between the users and interface, listened to their expression and recorded these details in my notebook, trying to find what confused the users, what dlighted the users, and what the value of Zhihu is.

After the Users Research, We found that people usually use our app when taking metro、staying on the bus、waiting in a queue or even staying in restroom, all in all , they used Zhihu App on mobile in pieces of time , and people love reading things they are interested in everyday. We need a clear way to present our valuable contents in history and updates. Thus, I have to make Zhihu app more clear and lightweight so that users could get the main idea of the information as soon as possible and be glad and patient to read more news and interesting stuff from Zhihu. Besides, we need to introduce who we are ,what we can bring to people in a clear and brief way to new users. So we got several directions to improve the experience of Zhihu App: navigation, layout, users guide, digging out more valuable contents.

As for the Layout

we refined the Answer pages and Article pages in order to get more space for reading. We tried to make full screen for users to read the contents.

As for the Users Guide

We drew 5 pages of illustrations with brief words to explain which features Zhihu has and what Zhihu can bring to our users.

Solution for Digging out more Valuable Contents:

We redesigned the Explore page to present more kinds of information including Hot Topics/ Hot Collections/ Editor's Choice/ Hot Charts, so users could find more contents they may be interested in or curious about out of Feed.

What is the Android Design from my perspective ?

Design exsits for people's need, it improves people's life and experience when they use an app or any tool. So I believe Android Design isn't only a rule for the Android Developers, but also a kind of spirit which could guide designers to create more apps with the innovation and consistency and satisfy people's needs in a better way.
